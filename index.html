Our story

Made, not manufactured on a whim.

Luli Co. started with a simple idea: hair clips shouldn't feel disposable. We work directly with a small accessories workshop to hand-select real flowers and seal them, petal by petal, into clips built to actually hold your hair — not just sit pretty in a drawer.

Every card that leaves our hands is packed by a small team who checks each piece before it ships. Because it's real flowers and hand-finished resin, slight variations in color and petal placement are part of what makes your clip yours.

— the Luli Co. team

Good to know

Questions, answered

Resin holds up well day to day, but avoid long soaks — towel it off if it gets wet. Keep clips out of direct sun, since prolonged light can soften petal color over time. The claw spring is sturdy but not indestructible — open it fully before clipping to avoid stressing the hinge.
